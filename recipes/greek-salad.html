Greek salad

Description

Greek salad is a traditional Mediterranean salad made with fresh ingredients such as tomatoes, cucumber, red onion, feta cheese, Kalamata olives, and a simple dressing of olive oil, lemon juice, and dried oregano.

This refreshing salad is known for its vibrant colors, tangy flavors, and healthy benefits. The combination of juicy tomatoes, crunchy cucumbers, and salty feta cheese makes Greek salad a perfect choice for a light lunch or a side dish for any meal.

Ingredients

  • Tomatoes
  • Cucumber
  • Red onion
  • Kalamata olives
  • Feta cheese
  • Extra virgin olive oil
  • Lemon juice
  • Dried oregano
  • Salt and pepper (optional)

Steps

  1. Chop the tomatoes and cucumber into bite-sized pieces.
  2. Slice the red onion thinly.
  3. Pit and chop the Kalamata olives.
  4. Crumble the feta cheese into small pieces.
  5. In a small bowl, whisk together the olive oil, lemon juice, dried oregano, and a pinch of salt and pepper to make the dressing.
  6. In a large bowl, combine the chopped vegetables, olives, and feta cheese.
  7. Pour the dressing over the salad and toss to coat.
  8. Chill the salad in the refrigerator for at least 30 minutes to allow the flavors to meld together.
  9. Just before serving, give the salad a final toss and adjust the seasoning with salt and pepper if needed.
  10. Serve and enjoy your homemade Greek salad!
