Baked beans on toast

Description

Baked beans on toast is a classic British dish made with thick slices of toast topped with a rich and flavorful tomato-based sauce with beans. The beans are usually haricot or cannellini beans that are cooked slowly with spices like paprika, cumin, and mustard.

Baked beans on toast is a popular breakfast or brunch food in many households and is often served with other accompaniments like crispy bacon, fried eggs, or grilled tomatoes. The dish is simple yet hearty and is perfect for a quick and satisfying meal.

Ingredients

  • 4 slices of bread
  • 1 can of baked beans in tomato sauce
  • 1 small onion, chopped
  • 1 garlic clove, minced
  • 1 tsp. paprika
  • 1 tsp. cumin
  • 1 tsp. mustard
  • 1 tbsp. olive oil
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Chopped fresh parsley (optional)

Steps

  1. Heat the olive oil in a pan over medium heat.
  2. Add the chopped onion and garlic and saut√© until translucent.
  3. Add the paprika, cumin, and mustard, and stir until fragrant.
  4. Add the can of baked beans to the pan and stir to combine with the spices.
  5. Turn the heat down to low, and let the beans simmer for 5-10 minutes to thicken the sauce.
  6. While the beans are simmering, toast the bread slices until lightly browned.
  7. Spoon the baked beans onto the toast, and season with salt and pepper to taste.
  8. Garnish with chopped fresh parsley, if desired.
  9. Serve hot and enjoy!
